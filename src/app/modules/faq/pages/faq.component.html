I am a Creator, and know other Creators. Is there a referral program with Chello?

Yes, there is a referral program with Chello. Every Creator will be given a referral code. In order to qualify for the referral program, the Creator has to pay the admin fee of ${{veriable.adminMonthlyFee}} per month or ${{veriable.adminYearlyFee}} annual. The Creator will be paid a 4% referral revenue every month on the 3rd, for the Creators they referred to Chello. The 4% is from the referred Creator business for the previous month. This is one half of the 6% Chello receives.

The referral fees are good for 24 months starting from when the referred Creator pays their first admin fee.

For example, Creator A refers to Creators B and C to Chello. In March, Creator B made $6000 and Creator C made $4000. On April 3, Creator A will have credited $200 referral revenue.
